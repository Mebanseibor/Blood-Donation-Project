Why TO Donate Blood

  • Curbs the risk of heart attack

    Excessive iron can be one of the resons for heart attack and increased cholesterol.blood donation maintain healthy iron levels.

  • Promotes weight loss

    The human body spends energy to regenerate the donated blood. thus,blood donation boosts metabolism and helps fat burn.

  • Strengthens immunity

    after a blood donation, your body gets into an activation mode faster, leading to strengthened immunity.

  • Reduces stress and related risks

    Overweight and stress are directly related. Thus, blood donation helps to kick away stress and its related risks like early aging and wrinkles.

  • Free health check-up

    Doctors test the overall health condition and history before a person can donate blood Thus, at a blood camp, a donor gets a free health and blood check-up.

  • Turn into a life saviour

    You will become life saviour by donating Blood.
